Circular 087

Welfare scholarship

Student are asked to apply for minority scholarship (Muslim, Parsi, Jain, Sikh, Buddhist, Christian) for the academic year 2024-2025.
Those who've failed to apply last year can apply for scholarship afresh this year through National Scholarship Portal.
Eligibility: income certificate (not exceeding 2 lakhs); community & nativity certificate; nationalized book account number, IFSC code, MICR number, Aadhaar card, bonafide certificate; for fresher: 50% of marks in grade 12, for renewal: 50% of marks in previous last 2 semester examinations.
Details for fresh applications:
The scheme is implemented through online scholarship management (OSMS).
It's mandatory for all students to apply online. The students shall apply on the scholarship website.
After submitting applications online, the students will take print-out of the application and submit the same to the college, without fail.
One copy of passport-size photograph with student signature; one self-attested copy of certificates, diploma, degree, etc. in respect of examinations passed; income & nativity certificate issued by state-authorized officer; proof for religion / community / transfer / self-declaration of community by student; copy of bank pass book, copy of fees receipt; copy of address proof
The last date of renewal is September 30, 2024.
